What is TEDxBerkeley?

On February 6, 2016, TEDxBerkeley is gathering world-thinkers, makers, and doers at UC Berkeley to discuss "Finding X". The audience will be composed of a diverse audience of over 2000 students, professors, and thought leaders from Berkeley and beyond for a stimulating day of presentations, discussions, entertainment and art that will spark new ideas and opportunities for all.
